Everything Bagel Dip

If you're a fan of everything bagels, you'll love this easy dip.

Prep time:5 min

Total time:5 min

Total:5 min

Ingredients:

Dip

  • 2/3 cup sour cream
  • 4 oz cream cheese; softened
  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons everything but the bagel seasoning; or to taste

Serving Options

  • bagel chips
  • flatbread
  • crackers
  • potato chips
  • vegetables

Steps:

  1. In food processor (or mixing bowl) combine sour cream, cream cheese, and mayonnaise. Mix until smooth, about 15-20 seconds. Scrape down sides of bowl, add in bagel seasoning. Blend another 10 seconds and taste, adding more seasoning as desired.
      2. *Let chill in fridge for 12+ hours. Flavors develop the longer it sits.
